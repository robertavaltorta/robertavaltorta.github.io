Roberta Rosa Valtorta

My research primarily focuses on dehumanization, with additional interests in gender-based violence, stereotypes, and inequality. More recently, I have been working on food sustainability.

I obtained my PhD in Social Psychology at the University of Milano-Bicocca (IT) in 2019, with a project on dehumanization. During my final PhD year, I continued my work at Panteion University (GR). From 2019 to 2024, I was a postdoc at the University of Valle d’Aosta (IT) and University of Milano-Bicocca, while also teaching social and group psychology at the University of Trento (IT).
