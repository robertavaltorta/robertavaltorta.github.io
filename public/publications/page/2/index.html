On the indirect link between virus anthropomorphism and health behavior: Evidence from the pandemic

By taking advantage of the COVID-19 pandemic, the present research investigates the implications of thinking about a virus in human-like terms for individuals’ health-related behaviors.

January 2024 · Roberta Rosa Valtorta, Noemi Orabona, Maria Paola Paladino

Investigating the link between biological dehumanization and indirect aggression

By considering the most dehumanized social groups in Italian society (e.g., homeless people, prisoners, and immigrants), this research explores the link between biologization (i.e., a form of dehumanization in which others are perceived as contagious entities) and indirect aggression.

December 2023 · Roberta Rosa Valtorta, Cristina Baldissarri, Chiara Volpato

Why might women justify dating violence? The role of men's sexual objectification of their romantic partners within heterosexual relationships

This research investigates the associations between men’s partner-sexual objectification, women’s self-objectification, and both partners’ attitudes toward dating violence.

May 2023 · Chiara Pecini, Daniela Ruzzante, Roberta Rosa Valtorta, Eleonora Crapolicchio, Veronica Margherita Cocco, Luca Andrighetto, Gian Antonio Di Bernardo

A twofold subjective measure of income inequality

This article introduces a twofold subjective Gini coefficient that combines perceptions of social class and pay structure to more accurately measure beliefs about income inequality.

May 2023 · Attila Gaspar, Carmen Cervone, Federica Durante, Anne Maass, Caterina Suitner, Roberta Rosa Valtorta, Michela Vezzoli

Gender stereotypes and sexualization in Italian children's television advertisements

This study examines gender stereotypes and sexualization in Italian television advertisements aimed at children. The findings reveal that gender stereotypes are still prevalent in Italian children’s television commercials and that girls are more sexualized than boys.

May 2023 · Roberta Rosa Valtorta, Cristina Baldissarri, Giuseppe Raguso, Giulia D'Ecclesiis, Chiara Volpato
